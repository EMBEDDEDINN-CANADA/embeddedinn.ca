what types of projects do you work on?

We work on a wide range of technical projects, from embedded firmware development to cloud infrastructure design. Our sweet spot is projects that require deep technical expertise and cross-domain knowledge.

We offer flexible engagement models including project-based, retainer, and team augmentation. Pricing depends on project scope, complexity, and timeline. We provide detailed estimates after initial consultation.

do you work with startups?

Yes, we work with companies of all sizes. For startups, we can provide technical guidance, help with architecture decisions, and build MVPs with a focus on scalability.

can you work with our existing team?

Absolutely. We frequently augment existing teams, providing specialized expertise while ensuring smooth collaboration and knowledge transfer.
